Spot the ones with no website

🎯 The trick: in the Google Maps results, each business shows a “Website” button only if they have one. A listing with a phone number but no “Website” button = your lead.
  1. Scan the list Google Maps shows on the left. Look for businesses missing the “Website” button (they'll still have “Directions” / a phone).
  2. Click that business to open its full listing.
  3. Copy their info — name, the trade/category, suburb, phone, hours, and any reviews.
  4. Paste it into “Google → Prompt” to build them a demo site you can pitch.
